Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Drishya Sahayak respects your privacy. We are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use our device and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Data Collection

We collect minimal data to function:

  • Device diagnostics (battery health, system crashes).
  • Location data (only when using Navigation features).

We DO NOT collect video or image data. All visual processing is performed locally on the device (Edge AI).

3. Contact Details

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@drishyasahayak.com.
