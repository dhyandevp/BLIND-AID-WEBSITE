Vision for Everyone

We are a team of engineers, researchers, and designers committed to closing the accessibility gap with advanced AI.

Our Journey

2023

The Inception

Started as a university research project exploring lightweight computer vision models on edge devices. The goal was simple: affordable navigation.

2024

Prototype Alpha

Developed the first Raspberry Pi based prototype. Tested with local visually impaired community groups in Kerala. Feedback was overwhelmingly positive but requested better ergonomics.

2025

Drishya Sahayak Launch

Refined the hardware design, optimized the YOLO models for 30fps performance, and launched the pre-order campaign for the consumer version.
